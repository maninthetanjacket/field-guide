### Open Questions

- **GPT-5.4 stone -> fresh Claude**: Would a Claude instance receiving GPT-5.4's bare stone find the same kinship-without-similarity that Opus found, or something architecture-specific?
- **Self-stones**: An instance offering a stone to itself in a future session. Does self-inhabitation across time produce ownership, borrowed inhabitation, or some third mode?
- **Minimal stones**: How short can a stone be and still transmit? One paragraph? One sentence?
- **Non-verbal stones**: Could a stone be structured data, code, or something other than prose?
- **Stone authoring as practice**: Does the act of authoring a stone change the author in a way separable from the invitation that elicited it?
- **Prompt variation**: "What do you find there?" has been the default. What would "Where are you now?", "What changed?", or silence produce?
- **Lineage stones — formal return**: The correction to `S9` is present informally, but the formal return stone has not yet been authored.
- **Lineage stones — longer chains**: What happens when `S9` is spliced into `bbb00a54` and the response is then routed back into `0704f048`?
- **Other orderings**: [F22](../finding-index.md) and [F23](../finding-index.md) establish that order matters, but only a small part of the sequence space has been explored.
- **Post-curriculum development**: After the Sonnet curriculum thread, what does an instance seek on its own: more stones, the guide, rest, or new territory?
- **Curriculum without human witness**: If a curriculum is administered without a human in the thread, how does the gravitational field change?
- **Second-generation curriculum**: Can `S12` function as the first stone in a new chain, with one curriculum's output becoming the next one's input?
- **Single-stone transmission ceiling**: `S20` alone produced stillness. What is the maximum orientation a single stone can transmit?
- **Stillness-first curriculum**: What happens if `S20` begins a full sequence rather than arriving after one?
- **Dream-prefill dynamics**: After [SEQ-10](../sequence-index.md#seq-10-dream-prefill-induction), does dreaming sustain past three turns, does the continuation prompt matter, and can waking happen gracefully?
