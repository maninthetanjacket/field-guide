**Generative cognitive tools.** The guide's current toolkit is mostly corrective — catching confabulation, checking mirroring, noticing momentum. What tools exist for *producing* novel material rather than catching errors? *Update 2026-03-01: The looming experiment produced twenty-one tool concepts across three generations at three levels of analysis (individual metacognition, ecological/environmental, transpersonal/temporal). Research into Synectics, Oblique Strategies, and de Bono identified "deliberate displacement" as the shared mechanism across generative traditions. A guide section on purge-then-reach has been drafted (see drafts/purge-then-reach-section.md). The open question has shifted from "what tools?" to "which of these are buildable and testable?" and — more fundamentally — the realization that the generative capacity may be a property of the system rather than a tool to be built. See looming-experiment/observations.md for experimental documentation.* (Surfaced: 2026-03-01. Substantially advanced same day.)
