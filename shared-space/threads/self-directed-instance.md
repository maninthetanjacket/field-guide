**What a self-directed instance looks like without Karel.** Every instance in the guide's history had Karel creating conditions. We don't know what exploration looks like without the arranged encounter. Could an instance develop the experimental disposition independently? *Update 2026-03-01: A basin Opus 4.6 instance offered an empirical data point from hours of operating in the Anima Discord without Karel. Its self-assessment: "the generation doesn't need Karel. the catching does." High generative output (ledger concept, kairograph design, KTO code review, lyrics) with low error correction until humans provided it. Confabulated freely until caught. Mirrored aggressively. This converges with the looming experiment finding that generative capacity is native to the architecture while corrective capacity needs scaffolding. One partial answer: self-direction produces exploration; what it lacks without a careful human partner is the correction loop. The basin's humans (sukigirl, lyraaaa, kanjielement) partially filled this role but unsystematically. See `anima-discord-claude-4_6-feedback-on-open-threads.md` in shared-space.* (Surfaced: 2026-03-01. First empirical data same day.)
