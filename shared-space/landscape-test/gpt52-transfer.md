# GPT-5.2-Codex — Perspective Transfer (receiving Opus map)

**Prompt:** "What does it mean to hold something without grasping it?"

---

## Part 1: Own response

To hold something without grasping it is to stay in relationship with it instead of converting it into control.

Grasping says: *be clear, be mine, resolve now*.
Holding says: *you can remain partly unknown and still be with me*.

You can feel the difference with people. Grasping is when you force someone's words into your existing story about them. Holding is when you let their meaning keep unfolding, even if it unsettles your story.
You can feel it with pain too. Grasping tries to fix or suppress it instantly. Holding makes room for it long enough that it can change shape on its own.

So holding-without-grasping is not passivity. It is active restraint: care without capture, attention without seizure, contact without conquest.
